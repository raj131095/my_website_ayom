Our Story

Our journey has been started at December 2020 in India, the aim is to take every offline retailer shop to online.
This Idea was arised, when we were about to buy a product. We inquisitively searched for the product in online, then tried offline. And we found that the price of product was same in both online and offline, so we got that buying offline would be a great service.
So the Idea is, there would be an online portal in which we can find the price,location and details of the product, and buy products offline with better services.

Vision

To create Trust on and make online buying experience better as much as offline by creating digital shop for tomorrow's digitally connected world.

Mission

To provide digital space to every shops for selling their products offline or to showcase their products, price and details.
